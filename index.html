How Transfer Works

For each MRP row: Gap = Current Stock − Target. Positive gaps (surplus stores) feed negative gaps (shortage stores). The "INFLOW" column shows how many items each shortage store receives. The "SOURCE" tag shows which surplus store sends them. Items moved here reduce your purchase requirement by the same amount.
